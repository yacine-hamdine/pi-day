Open Source Club

Open Source Club is a scientific club specialized in the field of technology and computer science in particular, it operates at the Faculty of Sciences - University of Algiers 1, for the benefit of students of all departments of the faculty.
Our club aims to encourage creativity and innovation in students by making their life more dynamic and full of learning.
We help you learn how to work with different software, be a leader, manage your time, and interact with lots of wonderful and amazing people.
We also support the students during their academic journey by helping them exchange knowledge and experience.
